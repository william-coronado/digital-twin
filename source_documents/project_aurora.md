# Project Aurora

Project Aurora is an initiative to build a sustainable solar energy microgrid for off-grid polar research stations.
It is scheduled to launch in Q4 2026.
The lead engineer is Dr. Sarah Jenkins.
The system uses high-density sodium-ion batteries capable of operating at temperatures down to -40 degrees Celsius.
The budget for the initial pilot is $2.4 million USD.
Reference Code: PA-2026-POLAR.
